# MindMirror

### Decode behavior. Predict reactions. Choose your move.

MindMirror is an AI-powered relationship strategist that analyzes real-life scenarios and provides clear, actionable insights into communication, emotional dynamics, and behavioral patterns.

Instead of promoting harmful behavior, MindMirror identifies toxic patterns such as gaslighting, guilt-tripping, and manipulation—then explains why they are harmful and offers healthier, more effective alternatives.

Built for hackathons, MindMirror transforms complex emotional situations into structured decisions users can understand and act on.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🧠 **Scenario Analysis**  
  Breaks down relationship situations into clear insights

- ⚠️ **Toxic Pattern Detection**  
  Identifies manipulation, gaslighting, and unhealthy behaviors

- 💬 **Smart Response Generator**  
  Suggests better ways to communicate effectively

- 🔮 **Outcome Simulation**  
  Predicts how different responses may play out

- 📊 **Behavioral Insights**  
  Explains why people might act a certain way

---

## 🧩 Example Use Cases

- “My partner ignores me after arguments. What should I do?”
- “They say I’m overreacting—am I being gaslighted?”
- “How do I communicate boundaries without causing conflict?”
- “What happens if I confront this directly?”
